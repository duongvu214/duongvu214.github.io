Auto Blur Face - Keep Photo Privacy

This application automatically detects faces in the selected image and suggests to anonymize them using one of the two censor options provided: Blur, Pixelate. It runs directly, locally on the device, it doesn't upload anything to any server. Moreover, users can define new blur zones manually to censor anything they want with a powerful editor. In addition, they can crop, edit, insert text, stickers to their photo.
